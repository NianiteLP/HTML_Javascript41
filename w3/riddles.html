Bio Education Riddles

What is black and white and read all over?

Up up up it goes and yet it never grows?

I am the son of water, but if water touches me I die. What am I?

What's strong enough to smash ships but still fears the sun?

A diamond plate, A glowing grate, A place you never leave. Where am I?

I am as strong as a rock, but a word can destroy me, What am I?

Without fingers, I point. Without arms, I strike. Without feet, I run. What am I?

What I want, the poor have, the rich need and if you eat you'll die, What am I?

The more you cut me, the bigger I grow. What am I?

A nightmare for some, for others a savior I come. My hands cold and bleak, it's the warm hearts they seek. Who am I?
